Who We Are

At Pine Health Foundation Limited, we are dedicated to redefining the healthcare system by prioritizing environmental stewardship and holistic care. We aim to provide equitable access to high-quality healthcare services while promoting education, sustainability, and community engagement. Through our patient-centered approach, we strive to foster a healthier and more knowledgeable community, where healthcare is accessible to all, guided by sustainable practices and collaborative partnerships. Join us as we lead toward a healthier, more sustainable future for individuals and the planet.

What We Do

Pine Health Foundation exists to provide comprehensive healthcare services and promote sustainable health development in Uganda and beyond. Besides our malnutrition project, we establish patient-centered healthcare systems that prioritize environmental sustainability and community engagement, provide equitable access to high-quality healthcare services, respecting the natural right to healthcare and prioritizing local needs and preferences, establish healthcare centers, hospitals, clinics, and dispensaries, including outreach centers and educational services, recruit medical volunteers and interns, including doctors, nurses, and medical students, to support our healthcare services, establish health institutions, universities, and training centers to provide health education and training, foster regional, national, and international partnerships to advance our healthcare mission and vision, develop sustainable business models to secure long-term funding and donations, conduct research and development to advance our healthcare services and minimize global warming's impact on health, implement sustainable practices, strategies, and policies to support our healthcare model, advocate for health rights, policies, and practices that promote a healthy and sustainable future, establish community pharmaceutical services and provide health-related services, and partner with health volunteers to establish hospitals, health centers, clinics, and health training schools.

Our Core Values

Patient-Centered Care

We prioritize the needs, preferences, and well-being of our patients, ensuring compassionate and personalized care.

Integrity

We uphold the highest honesty, ethics, and professionalism standards in all our interactions and endeavors.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in healthcare delivery, continuous improvement, and innovation to achieve the best possible patient and community outcomes.

Collaboration

We foster a culture of teamwork, collaboration, and mutual respect among healthcare providers, staff, volunteers, and community partners.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions, decisions, and performance,

ensuring transparency and accountability in all aspects of our work.

Environmental stewardship and holistic care

Integrating environmental consciousness into healthcare aims to create a harmonious relationship between people and the planet, for a healthier and more sustainable future.
